JoJo's Bizarre Campaign Concept

# Lobby Place
- Start in a lobby world, can roam freely
- Bottom, button navigation for menus
  - Party
  - Player
  - Goals
  - Shop
  - Logs

## Party Menu
- Pop-up menu, contains a list of joinable parties
- Search bar at the bottom for searching usernames to join their party
- Two buttons, One to create a party, another to close the menu
### Party Creation Menu
- Set the party size with arrows, 1-5 players
- Toggle for friends only
- Difficulty slider, Easy / Normal / Hard / Nightmare
### Party Waiting Menu
- A section for displaying the active party members
- Two buttons below, One to disband, another to Start (can start without the party full)
- Right side contains a list of modifiers, to make the game harder for extra cash and exp

## Player Menu
- Hides the other buttons, full screen usage
- Buttons at the top, Customization, Classes, Perks
- Button at the bottom, Finish (close menu)
### Customization Menu
- Center stays open to see player
- Left side contains list with options for customization
  - Contains skin color section (set selections), hair color section (set selections)
  - Contains face options, eyebrows, eyes eye color (set selections), nose, mouth
  - Contains a button for outfits, which brings up a popup menu and list
  - Contains a button for accessories which brings up a popup menu and list
- Right side contains Hair IDs, or color wheel depending on option
### Classes Menu
- Left side contains a list of classes to select and choose
- Right side contains a text box with descriptions
### Perks Menu
- A central popup menu, containing a list of equippable perks
- Lefts side is descriptions, middle is perk lost, right is equipped perks (up to 5)

## Goals Menu
- Popup menu, contains a list with boxes with achievements/goals
- Button at the bottom to close

## Shop Menu
- Popup menu, contains a list of purchasable products/passes
- Button at the bottom to close

## Logs Menu
- Popup menu, contains a list with boxes with update logs
- Button at the bottom to close

# Game Place
- Players teleport to a private game instance with their party.
- They will automatically get their perks, classes, etc. equipped and setup.

## General Gameplay
- Players will be lined up against an enemy team in combat encounters 
- The camera will always be at a set location, no camera movement
- There will be multiple background sets, which can be changed at any moment to fit the combat encounters
- Just as combat, during rest encounters, same system, set camera, and a set background scene, with positions to place the party players

## Combat Scene
- 5 slots for players on the left, 5 slots for enemies on the right
- Bottom middle contains information and buttons, such as health, stamina, and active effects 
- Combat is turn based, when its your turn, your healthbar area will push and up and show 3 new buttons: Fight/Item/Rest
- Combat section contains a list of abilities, determined on your class, level, and other factors determined ingame
- Items section contains a list of consumables from your inventory
- Rest section uses up your turn to generate more energy

## Rest Scene
- Contains 5 slots to place party members
- Contains a central menu
- Contains tabs, for items, abilities, and crafting
- Item tab contains a list of all your items in your inventory, with a description box at the bottom for the selected item
- Ability tab contains a list of your abilities, with a description box at rhe bottom for the selected ability
- Crafting tab contains a list of items you have at least 1 item for the recipe to craft, and a description box at the bottom for the selected item
- Left side contains a stash that players can add items to, to share them with their party
- Right side contains a section with your equipment slots, and stats
- Bottom contains a button to Ready, to move on to the next encounter when a majority is ready

## Encounters
- Once moving on from a rest scene, players vote for their next encounter 
- Random selection of 3 different encounters, and two extra buttons to either scavenge and area for items, or rest to regain more hp or train
- Encounters start with scenes and text boxes, players voting on chat options choosing how the encounter goes
- At the end of every encounter is a battle scene, either fighting specific enemies due to the encounter option, or random enemies if they finish the encounter peacefully

## Stats
- Broken into two sections, Body Stats, and Mind Stats
- Body Stats: Strength, Health, Defense
- Mind Stats: Intelligence, Spirit, Willpower
- Stats affect different skills depending on your class, and can provide certain checks in encounters to change the outcome
- Health and Defense provide direct bonuses to the player, regardless of class
- Strength affects the power of more physical oriented abilities, can be used in encounter checks
- Spirit affects the power of more supernatural related abilities, and csn be used in certain encounters checks 
- Intelligence affects ability accuracy, dodge chance/dodge negation, and other factors of certain skills, is used for encounter cheks
- Willpower affects critical chances, and other factors of certain skills, and is used in encounters checks
### Stat Box
- The menu in rest area where your stats are listed, shows your information and bonuses
- Also shows other information, like class, abilities, stand, etc.

## Equipment 
- Players have multiple equipment slots to put gear they find, buy, or craft
- Helmet / Chest / Pants / Boots
- Weapon / Charms x3
- Can be equipped and unequipped from the slots, also hidden visually 
- Provide stat bonuses, and sometimes passives, as well as active skills
